What type of loan are you looking for ?

Considering a loan? BPR offers different types of loans for different types of banking needs for both Personal growth and development in Agricultural fields. Our loan schemes are simple, convenient and straightforward tailor made for you and your financial requirements.

CAR LOAN

A BPR Car Loan is designed to finance clients who want to buy a car for personal use. Tenor is up to 5 years.
Both new and used cars are financed.
For used cars the manufacturing period considered is 10 years.
Competitive interest rates

Home Equity

Home Equity Loans are secured Medium to Long Term loans to BPR customers with stable monthly Income that are designed to suit personal needs. Max amount is Rwf 100Mn
No customer’s contribution.
Bank’s financing is up to 50% of the property to be refinanced.
Tenor is up to 10 years if the loan is for investment and up to 5 years if the use is for consumption.
Monthly repayment is up to 50% of the net salary.

PERSONAL LOAN

Personal Banking Loans are secured or unsecured loans to finance BPR Clients to meet their personal financial needs. Tenor is up to 5 years
Unsecured loan financing is up to FRW 15,000,000
Competitive interest rates

Home Loan

A BPR Home Loan is designed to finance clients who want to buy or build a house.
Tenor is up to 25 years
Financing for construction of a new or a ready built house
Competitive interest rates
