Executive Management

+
Maurice Toroitich
Executive Director
Prior to his current appointment, Maurice Toroitich held a similar role in KCB Bank Rwanda Limited over a period of 8 years. During this period, he led KCB Group entry into the Rwanda market and established KCB Bank Rwanda Ltd as a Greenfield operation by effectively championing the development of a wide array of market driven products and services leading to the acquisition of a 9% market share within the 8-year period. Prior to joining KCB Group, Maurice had a long career of 15 years with Stanbic Bank Kenya Ltd where he held various senior assignments in Corporate Banking, Retail and Credit. Maurice has been engaged in several projects in the financial services area including the formation process of Stock Exchanges in Rwanda and the regulations of Capital Markets Authority in Rwanda. He has been associated with several landmark transactions in sectors such as telecommunications, power, consumer, food products and airlines in different countries. He provided leadership in pioneering the introduction of electronic payment systems, Agency Banking, SME financing programs, entrepreneur development facilities and other important financial initiatives. Maurice has worked along with governments, private and public sector, DFI’s and multilateral agencies on handling sovereign debt issues, infrastructure financing and other development initiatives in Eastern Africa. Maurice has been the Chairman of Rwanda Bankers Association for two years and Chairman of the Board of Trustees of Stanbic Bank Staff Pension and Life Assurance Scheme from 2004-2008. He is a certified Stanbic Bank’s Balanced Score Card (BSC) trainer. Maurice holds a Master of Business Administration degree from Strathmore Business School, he also holds a Bachelor of Commerce (Accounting Option) from the University of Nairobi. He is an Associate of the Chartered Institute of Bankers, London (ACIB-London) and a Certified Public Accountant - CPA (K).
+
Afrique Ramba
Chief Operating Officer
RAMBA is an experienced Banker, Investment and Finance Professional with a track record of success in Investment Banking, he is a seasoned investment executive with experience spanning over ten years in some of Rwanda’s biggest public institutions, he currently is the Chief Operating Officer of Banque populaire Du Rwanda Plc. Prior to that, he served as the Deputy Director General in charge of Funds Management at Rwanda Social Security Board (RSSB) where his role arose out of his stellar performance as the Acting Director General of the Social Security Fund of Rwanda (SSFR). While at SSFR, he was responsible for steering the overall management of the fund. RAMBA managed the institution to prepare its transition to merge it with the government medical scheme. Prior to that, he was the Director of Investments and Special Projects where he was responsible for managing SSFR’s $300 million investment portfolio across a number of asset classes. He helped to establish, grow and modernize the Investment Group from its nascent stage, resulting to several years of good performance. Previously, he worked as a Bank Inspector in the National Bank of Rwanda where he used his accounting and audit background during forensic and statutory audits of several commercial banks as well as advising them on regulation compliance. RAMBA has also served as the Chairman on a number of Boards including Banque Populaire du Rwanda Plc, CIMERWA; the biggest cement manufacturer in Rwanda, Rwanda Enterprise Investment Company (REIC), a venture capital fund and Building Materials Investments Company, one of the biggest construction material companies in Rwanda and Mount Meru Soyco Ltd, a regional oil manufacturing company. He also served as a member of the Rwanda Stock Exchange Index Committee. RAMBA is a member of the Chartered Certified Accountant (ACCA-UK) and holds a Master’s degree, chartered Bankers MBA from Prifysgol Bangor University and a Bachelor’s degree in Economics from National University of Rwanda.

Godwin AKANKUNDA
Country Head of Internal Audit
Godwin AKANKUNDA joined Banque populaire Du Rwanda Plc. in July 2017 as the country Head of Audit. He began his career in February 2001 as an Auditor in the Office of the Auditor General for State Finances, Rwanda. He rose through the ranks where he was appointed as the Assistant Auditor General for State Finances of Rwanda in January 2017. Godwin is a member of ACCA, Institute of Certified Public Accountants, Rwanda (iCPAR), He holds an MBA from Oklahoma Christian University, USA and a bachelors’ degree in Management from the National University of Rwanda, presently known as the University of Rwanda. He has participated in various finance management trainings offered by the UK National Audit Office, US Government Accountability Office (Fellowship Programme), AFROSAI-E South Africa, among others.
